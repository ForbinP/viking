  • CHARACTERS
  • STORY

  • The Viking - The Viking is the games’ main character that the player controls to solve puzzles.


  • Sheep – A peaceful animal that watches the Viking with curious eyes. The Viking can shoot sheep to turn it into a snowball for a few seconds and move it around.


  • Heart – When the Viking walks through a heart, he also gets a bullet. To finish the level, the Viking has to collect all the hearts, which opens up the treasure box.


  • The Treasure Box – At first the treasure box is closed but after the Viking collects all the hearts the box opens and the treasure is revealed. The Viking can then walk through the treasure box and collect the treasure.


  • The Door – At first the door is closed but after the Viking has collected all the hearts and the treasure, the door opens. When the Viking walks through the door the level is complete.


  • Moveable Block – The Viking can move the block around as needed. The rune on the block is “Ægishjálmur”, which is a magical rune that protects you from evil


  • Puffin – It walks around in a clockwise circle until it collides with the Viking, at which point it falls asleep.


  • Troll – It will kill the Viking if it has an unobstructed view of him. It will first bare its teeth menacingly when the Viking is halfway into his line of sight, and shoots the Viking if he fully steps into his view


  • Polar Bear – Another peaceful animal that watches the Viking. The Viking can shoot a polar bear and turn it into a snowball for a few seconds and move it around.


  • Dragon – At first, all dragons are asleep. After all the hearts have been collected the dragon will wake up and breathe fire when the Viking is in the same line to its left.


  • Dragon – At first, all dragons are asleep. After all the hearts have been collected the dragon will wake up and breathe fire when the Viking is in the same line to its right.


  • Lásabrjótur – Is a rune that is at the end of the first level theme, “The farm”. It’s a rune that opens a lock without a key


  • Vegvísir – Is a rune that is at the end of the second level theme, “Cold winter”. It’s a rune that guides through bad weather.


  • Gibu Auja – Is a rune that is at the end of the third level theme, “Lagoon”. It’s a rune that gives good luck


  • Stafur til að vekja upp draug – Is a rune that is at the end of the final level theme, “Volcano”. It’s a rune that wakes up ghost and evil spirit.
